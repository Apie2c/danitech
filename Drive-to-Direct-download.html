What is a Google Drive Direct Download Link?

A Google Drive Direct Download Link allows recipients to download files directly from Google Drive without having to visit the Drive interface or click through several prompts. This streamlined process is particularly useful when you want to share files quickly and efficiently, or when sharing with people who may not be familiar with navigating Google Drive.

Why Use a Direct Download Link?
